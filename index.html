A graphic designer is a professional who creates visual concepts, using computer software or by hand, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, or captivate consumers. They develop the overall layout and production design for various applications such as advertisements, brochures, magazines, and corporate reports.
A VFX artist is a professional who creates visual effects for film, television, and video games. They use a variety of techniques, including 3D animation, compositing, and motion graphics, to enhance live-action footage and create realistic and believable imagery.
A motion graphic designer is a professional who creates animated designs and visual effects for film, television, and digital media. They use animation software and tools such as Adobe After Effects, Cinema 4D and Maya to create dynamic, animated graphics and special effects. They are skilled in visual storytelling and able to create engaging, dynamic and memorable visuals that help to tell a story and make it more engaging.
A colorist is a professional who specializes in the color correction and color grading of visual media such as films, television shows, and commercials. They work in post-production, and use various software and tools to adjust the color, brightness, contrast, and saturation of images and videos.
A photographer is an artist who uses a camera to capture still images. They use various techniques such as composition, lighting, and exposure to create visually striking and evocative photographs. Photographers can specialize in different areas such as portrait, landscape, commercial, editorial, wedding, and fine art.
A music composer is an individual who creates original music by writing and arranging compositions for various forms of media, such as films, television shows, video games, and concert performances. They often work in collaboration with other professionals such as conductors, performers, and sound engineers to bring their compositions to life.
